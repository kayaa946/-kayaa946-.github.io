BIRTHING DOLL

Created By:

Japanese Toy

Year:

18th-19th Century

Why?:

This particular doll was a very popular attraction during the 18th Century among the residents of Edo. It is commonly believed these dolls were initially made to teach midwives how to deliver babies; evidence suggests that they were used for entertaining purposes.

But Why??:

Because what is more fun than pushing a baby out of yourself?!
