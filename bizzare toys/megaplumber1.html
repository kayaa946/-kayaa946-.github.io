MEGA PLUMBER

Created By:

American Standard

Year:

2001

Why?:

Mega Plumber action figure, created by American Standard (a company that also makes toilets) to help kids “see plumbers as true superheroes.” The six-inch figure comes equipped with his own monkey wrench, miniature toilet, and—so important for superheroes—rubber gloves.

But Why??:

Because he does come to your rescue and you know it!
