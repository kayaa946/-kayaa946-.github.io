Created By:

Gary Dahl

Year:

1975

Why?:

Rock is a collectible conceived in 1975 by advertising executive Gary Dahl. Pet Rocks are smooth stones from Mexico's Rosarito Beach. They were marketed like live pets,in custom cardboard boxes,complete with straw and breathing holes.
